# 🎨 NayePankh Foundation - UI/UX Homepage Design System

## 🌟 1. Design System (Brand Identity)
* **Primary Colors:** * `#FF9933` (Saffron/Orange) - Represents Energy, Hope, and Giving Wings.
  * `#138808` (Green) - Represents Growth, Youth, and NGO's Core Mission.
* **Secondary Colors:** `#FFFFFF` (Clean White) and `#222222` (Dark Charcoal for readability).
* **Typography (Fonts):** * Headings: `Poppins` or `Montserrat` (Bold & Modern)
  * Body Text: `Inter` or `Roboto` (Clean & Clean for Mobile users)
