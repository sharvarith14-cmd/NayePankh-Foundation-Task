Our Mission

To ensure no student is held back by lack of opportunity, guidance, or resources. We believe every young mind deserves a fair chance to fly.

NayePankh Foundation works at the grassroots level, connecting students with mentors, internships, and skill-building programs that open real doors.

📚

Education Support

Free study materials, doubt sessions and career counselling for underprivileged students.

💻

Internship Programs

Real-world internships that help students gain skills and confidence before graduation.

🤝

Volunteer Network

A community of 1200+ volunteers who give their time to make a lasting difference.
